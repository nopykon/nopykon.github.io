Listening

My favorite genres would probably have to be Classical or Rock, but there is so much brilliant stuff out there in lots of genres that it's hard to pick a specific one. Games, and movies, do include some of the best music I've ever heard. That said, not many soundtracks have impressed me lately, because modern well produced accompanying music often lack identity, in my opinion. Some just have no melody, almost pure ambience. Most AAA-games are trying for the Hollywood-style (which can be great!), but not doing the best job at it, resulting in something that doesn't sound bad, but not interesting or memorable either. But that is how it's always going to be. For anyone's taste, the exceptionally good stuff will be released far and few between. And finding it among the insane amount of available content of today will also happen rarely. Probably some of my future favorites, have already been out for years, if not decades (or even centuries now that I think about it).

The demoscene produces a lot of good stuff. I often tune in to scenesat.com for some of that. I also have gigantic folder with .mod and .xm files by artists like Maktone, Dubmood, Mosaik/Rymdlego, Cerror.... and too many others for me to keep track of. Often, I only know the tune, not the artist.
