First Person Shooter

Work in progress

Pitch (WIP)

Kill the bad guys, find the exit.
 It's a first person shooter with emphasis on combat, exploration and atmosphere.

The extraterrestrials conspire to erase humanity from the surface of the earth. It falls upon you, the player, to stop them. You must visit their many cells, scattered around the globe, to disrupt their operation and destroy the life-threatening equipment they plan to use against us.

Features

  • Fun, challenging and rewarding combat loop.
  • Navigation and exploration through beautiful low-poly environments high in variability (some levels aren't even that grey-brown).
  • Five enemy types with well tweaked senses, extensive pathfinding skills and expressive animation to reveal intent and morale. Additional bosses and nonhostile creatures.
  • Plenty of weapons. Handguns, machine guns, shotguns, grenades and rockets. And the arsenal of the enemy. All with different strengths and weaknesses.
  • A concise experience. No unskippable cutscenes or other bloat. The game tries hard not to waste your time.
  • Vomitingly Beautiful Visuals. PS1 stylized graphics achieved through Software Rendering (OpenGL Hardware Renderer in the works, functional but with some minor glitches).
  • Affine texture-mapping and correction via on the fly subdivision.
  • Baked and dynamic per-vertex lighting.
  • Levels consisting of up to (checks source code) ...3072 polygons!
  • All models and textures made exclusively for this game.
  • Amazing particles and effects that adds to the mood and the carnage.
