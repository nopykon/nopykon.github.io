3D Modelling

I begun my 3D modelling journey attemting to make maps for Counter-Strike 1.6 using Hammer (aka. "WorldCraft"). Since then I've used many different tools, but in recent years almost exclusively Blender.

Those who know me will know that I complain about Blender... a lot. There are some things that annoy me about the program and it's development, but in the end; It's a great piece of software and I'm very happy that it exists.

Low Poly

Most of my recent work has been in a low poly style, inspired by PS1-era games. Something about capturing life with just a few dozen polys brings me joy.
